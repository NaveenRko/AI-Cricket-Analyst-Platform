1. Virat Kohli & AB de Villiers - 229 Runs (2016)
Teams: Royal Challengers Bengaluru vs Gujarat Lions
Explanation: This remains the highest partnership ever recorded in the tournament. During a league match at the M. Chinnaswamy Stadium, the duo put on a mammoth 229-run stand for the 2nd wicket. Kohli smashed 109 runs, and De Villiers remained unbeaten on 129, propelling RCB to a massive 248/3 and a comfortable 144-run victory.

2. KL Rahul & Nitish Rana - 220 Runs (2026)
Teams: Delhi Capitals vs Punjab Kings
Explanation: Displayed in a sensational display of batting at the Arun Jaitley Stadium, KL Rahul and Nitish Rana forged an unbeaten 220-run partnership. Rahul scored an incredible 152 not out (the highest individual score by an Indian in the IPL), while Rana contributed a vital 91 to help set an imposing target.

3. Virat Kohli & AB de Villiers - 215* Runs (2015)
Teams: Royal Challengers Bengaluru vs Mumbai Indians
Explanation: The legendary duo appears on this list for a second time thanks to their explosive 2nd-wicket stand at the Wankhede Stadium. After losing Chris Gayle early, Kohli (82*) and De Villiers (133*) completely dismantled the Mumbai bowling attack, leading RCB to a massive 235/1 and an eventual 39-run win.

4. Shubman Gill & Sai Sudharsan - 210 Runs (2024)
Teams: Gujarat Titans vs Chennai Super Kings
Explanation: This 1st-wicket stand was a masterful run-chase performance at the Narendra Modi Stadium. Shubman Gill scored 104 and Sai Sudharsan hit 100, as the duo became only the second pair in IPL history to score hundreds in the same innings, successfully chasing a massive target of 232.

5. KL Rahul & Quinton de Kock - 210* Runs (2022)
Teams: Lucknow Super Giants vs Kolkata Knight Riders
Explanation: The pair put together an unbeaten 210-run opening stand in a must-win league match at the DY Patil Stadium. Both openers were in magnificent touch, with Quinton de Kock scoring 140* and KL Rahul contributing 68*, allowing LSG to post 210/0 and win a nail-biter by 2 runs
