The absolute fastest fifties in Indian Premier League history were both achieved in just 13 deliveries.

1. Yashasvi Jaiswal – 13 Balls
Team: Rajasthan Royals (RR)
Opponent: Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR)
Year: 2023
Details: Jaiswal set the all-time tournament record during a run-chase at Eden Gardens. He scored 26 runs in the opening over and reached his half-century in just 13 balls, surpassing the previous 14-ball record. He finished unbeaten on 98 off 47 balls.

2. Urvil Patel – 13 Balls
Team: Chennai Super Kings (CSK)
Opponent: Lucknow Super Giants (LSG)
Year: 2026
Details: Patel equalled Jaiswal's 13-ball record during a blistering knock for CSK. His whirlwind innings included two fours and eight massive sixes, helping set a new tone for the team.

3. KL Rahul – 14 Balls
Team: Punjab Kings (PBKS)
Opponent: Delhi Capitals (DC)
Year: 2018
Details: Rahul brought up his 50 in just 14 balls, hitting six fours and four sixes to record the fastest half-century of the 2018 season and setting a benchmark that stood for five years.

4. Pat Cummins – 14 Balls
Team: Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR)
Opponent: Mumbai Indians (MI)
Year: 2022
Details: Known primarily as a fast bowler, Cummins tied the then-fastest 50 record with a stunning display of power-hitting. He smashed four fours and six sixes to lead KKR to victory.

5. Romario Shepherd – 14 Balls
Team: Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB)
Opponent: Chennai Super Kings (CSK)
Year: 2025
Details: Shepherd showcased his late-game finishing skills, bringing up his fifty in only 14 balls against arch-rivals CSK at the M. Chinnaswamy Stadium.

6. Yusuf Pathan – 15 Balls
Team: Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR)
Opponent: Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH)
Year: 2014
Details: In a crucial match where KKR needed a massive win to secure the second spot in the playoffs, Pathan took apart the SRH bowling attack, scoring 50 off 15 balls and finishing with 72 runs.

7. Sunil Narine – 15 Balls
Team: Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR)
Opponent: Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB)
Year: 2017
Details: Serving as a pinch-hitter for KKR, Narine dismantled the RCB bowling unit, equaling Pathan’s 15-ball milestone in a quickfire 54-run innings.

8. Nicholas Pooran – 15 Balls
Team: Lucknow Super Giants (LSG)
Opponent: Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB)
Year: 2023
Details: Chasing a massive 213, Pooran came in with LSG in a tough spot. He tore apart the RCB bowling to reach his fifty in 15 balls, eventually scoring 62 off 19 deliveries to win the match for his team.

9. Jake Fraser-McGurk – 15 Balls
Team: Delhi Capitals (DC)
Opponent: Sunrisers Hyderabad / Mumbai Indians
Year: 2024
Details: The explosive young Australian batter proved his aggressive intent by blasting a 15-ball fifty twice during the 2024 season.

10. Vaibhav Sooryavanshi – 15 Balls
Team: Rajasthan Royals (RR)
Opponent: Chennai Super Kings (CSK) / Sunrisers Hyderabad
Year: 2026
Details: As one of the youngest talents to break into the IPL, this teenager smashed a 15-ball fifty in his explosive breakthrough 2026 season
