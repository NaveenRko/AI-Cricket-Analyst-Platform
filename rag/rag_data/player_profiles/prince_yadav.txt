Prince Yadav (born 10 June 1996) is an Indian cricketer who plays as a right-arm fast-medium bowler. He plays for Delhi in domestic cricket and Lucknow Super Giants in the Indian Premier League (IPL).

Early life
Prince Yadav was born on 10 June , 1996 in Delhi, India. Growing up in a family that encouraged sports, he began playing cricket at a young age and was enrolled in a local cricket academy at age 12 to focus on fast bowling.
In December 2019, the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) banned Yadav from domestic cricket for two seasons after he was found guilty of age fudging .Yadav had submitted a birth certificate claiming he was born on 12 December 2001.
However, after investigating a complaint, the BCCI cross-checked his records with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), which confirmed he passed his Class X exams in 2012 and that his actual date of birth is 10 June 1996.

Domestic career
Yadav rose through the ranks of Delhi's junior cricket system, representing the Delhi Under-19 team in 2018. He made his first-class debut for the senior Delhi team in January 2024 during a Ranji Trophy match against Jammu and Kashmir.
He made his List A debut for Delhi in the 2024–25 Vijay Hazare Trophy, where he notably took 4/53 against Tripura. His performances in the Delhi Premier League T20, where he took 13 wickets in 10 matches for the New Delhi Tigers, were pivotal in earning him attention from IPL franchises.

Indian Premier League
Yadav was signed by the Lucknow Super Giants for his base price of ₹30 lakh during the IPL 2025 auction, having previously served as a net bowler for the team. He made his IPL debut on 24 March 2025 against Delhi Capitals.
During his debut season, he claimed his first IPL wicket by bowling Travis Head in a match against Sunrisers Hyderabad. In the 2026 IPL season, he recorded his best bowling figures of 2/20 against Delhi Capitals. As of April 2026, he has taken 9 wickets in 10 IPL matches.

International Career
Yadav got his maiden International call up for the ODI series vs Afghanistan.
